## Overview

TapKit uses iOS accessibility features (Switch Control) to execute gestures on your iPhone. This requires some initial configuration.

## Requirements

- iPhone running iOS 16 or later
- USB cable
- Mac with TapKit app installed

## Setup Steps

1. Connect your iPhone to your Mac via USB
2. Trust the Mac when prompted on your iPhone
3. Enable Switch Control when prompted by TapKit
4. Allow required permissions

## Switch Control

Switch Control is an iOS accessibility feature that TapKit uses to execute touch gestures. TapKit automatically configures Switch Control when you connect your phone.

## Permissions

Your iPhone may request permissions for:
- USB data connection
- Trusting the connected Mac
